Chocolate cake with vanilla/almond frosting provides a
tasty treat for any ocassion. Follow the directions for making 
any flavour of chocolate cake. While the cake bakes in the oven, 
prepare a box of vanilla frosting mix added with roasted and
buttered almond silvers. Evenly coated in melted butter the
almond silvers toast in a hot skillit for approximately ten 
minutes. Thoroughly toast the silvers without turning them 
crisp.Cool the toasted almonds and set aside a handful for 
the final touch. Fold the almonds into the vanilla frosting, and
after covering the cake, sprinkle a handful of toasted almonds
on the top. After serving this desert to your guests.
